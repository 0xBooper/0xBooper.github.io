(Note: The USB drive can be found at https://www.amazon.ca/EMTEC-ECMMD64GB253-Flash-Drive-Slide/dp/B01N24H1NU.)

This drive was given to me as a birthday gift, by the way.

This "Usb 3.0" drive is WAY slower than normal 3.0 speeds. At best it could be a USB 2.0 drive, but definitely not a USB 3.0 drive.

Speeds:

The highest I got was 32mb per second, on a USB 2.0 port. On a USB 3.0 port, it sits around the same.

Sometimes it can get REALLY slow, like 32kkb per second.

Overall, I can't recommend this. If you're on a budget, and you don't care about the speed (and have some time to spare), I suppose this may be good.
