What is the Bash Scripting Utility?

The Bash Scripting Utility is a utility made by me (0xBooper) to help create and setup bash scripts easily.

What do you mean by setup?

By setup, I mean it automatically makes the script executable, and it puts "#!/bin/bash" into the file.
