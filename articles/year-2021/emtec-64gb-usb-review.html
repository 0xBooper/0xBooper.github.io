I got this drive as a birthday gift, and here are my thoughts about it.

Soo, this "Usb 3.0" drive is WAY slower than normal 3.0 speeds. At best it could be a USB 2.0 drive, but definitely not a USB 3.0 drive.

Speeds: (this wsas measured by transferring a 1gb file to and from the internal hard disk.)

The highest speed I got was 32mb per second, on a usb 2.0 port, NOT 3.0. (This was when I transferred the said 1gb file to the internal drive.

And it got so slow, that it got down to around 32KB per second.

If you don't care about speed at all, and your pretty patient, I guess this could be a good drive. Otherwise, spend your money on something else that's way better.
